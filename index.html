  Who is Teacher?  

     A teacher, also called a schoolteacher or formally an educator, is a person who helps students to acquire knowledge, competence, or virtue.The primary role of a teacher is to deliver classroom instruction that helps students learn. To accomplish this, teachers must prepare effective lessons, grade student work and offer feedback, manage classroom materials, productively navigate the curriculum, and collaborate with other staff.

     Although how much define the word "teacher" in any dictionary, as for me "teacher" is the most invaluable word for my life because they can change the image of my life.

  The Childhood Teachers
